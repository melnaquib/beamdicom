D↑COM receives DICOM images either by an internal router service, drag and drop DICOM images onto our app or choose a folder of images to convert. The images can be converted into PDF, JPG, PNG or BMP images. You can save the newly converted images in a main folder with a location and name of your choice. Subfolders will automatically be created with the patient’s name inside the main folder. The user can brand all the images with your name and logo.
